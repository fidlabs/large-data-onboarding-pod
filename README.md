# Large Data Onboarding (LDO) System

The Large Data Onboarding (LDO) System is a dedicated pathway for organizations seeking Filecoin DataCap allocations for large-scale storage onboarding cases that cannot be reasonably served through standard allocation pathways.

This process is intended for organizations that are willing to undergo enhanced due diligence in order to demonstrate:

- why existing automated or standard allocator pathways are not suitable for their use case,
- the legitimacy of the underlying business activity,
- the authenticity of storage demand and associated payments,
- and that no self-dealing, circular arrangements, or other abusive behaviors are taking place.

Applications are reviewed through a combination of:

- business and organizational information,
- contracts and commercial agreements,
- proofs of payment and financial flows,
- technical architecture and onboarding plans,
- and any additional supporting documentation required by the review team.

The goal of the LDO System is to support legitimate large-scale onboarding while maintaining high standards of transparency, accountability, and ecosystem integrity.

## Applying

To apply, please open a GitHub issue using the provided application template.

If access to confidential documentation is required, the LDO Pod is willing to execute appropriate NDAs in order to review sensitive business information.
